The pedagogy

Four moves, every lesson, no exceptions.

The same retention loop runs across every phase of every course — the math and physiology before the software button, and a keepable artifact at the end of it.

move 1

Learn

The concept from first principles — the equation or mechanism before any tool touches it.

move 2

Build

Implement it hands-on in R — mrgsolve, nlmixr2, or NONMEM control-stream syntax where relevant.

move 3

Ship

Walk away with a reusable artifact: a model template, a decision checklist, a diagnostic script.

move 4

Check

Three short questions gate the next lesson — no advancing on a false sense of understanding.
